\section{Chronogram}
\label{sec:chronogram}

This section outlines the planned development schedule for the project. The work is broken down into five distinct phases, mapping the key implementation, demonstration, and release tasks to a five-month timeline from July 2025 to November 2025. This chronogram serves as a high-level project plan to guide and track development progress.
